Over 500 Font Awesome icons is included with Blogr-AMP Blogger template with Accelerated Mobile Pages HTML hosted by [MaxCDN](https://maxcdn.bootstrapcdn.com/)Â -- Content Delivery Network.  
  
Earlier in [Blogr-AMP V1](https://blogr-amp.blogspot.com/search/label/V1), this theme uses Material icons powered by Google Fonts. However, due to the many request by users (we heard you!), we opted out of this font library & use [Font Awesome](https://www.blogger.com/) web icons instead.  
  
[Font Awesome icons](http://fontawesome.io/) are beautifully crafted, delightful, & easy to use in your web, Android, and iOS projects. Learn more about Font Awesome icons in their [Getting Started guide](http://fontawesome.io/get-started/) and view all available icons to choose at their [Icons selection page](http://fontawesome.io/icons/).  
  
Add icons to your blog made easy!
